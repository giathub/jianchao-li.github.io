Biography

I am Jianchao Li, a software engineer specialized in computer vision. I received my bachelor's degree from the University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB) in 2013 and my master's degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2016. During my master study, I worked in the Visual Information Processing and Learning (VIPL) group at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS) with Professor Shiguang Shan, on applying deep learning to computer vision and completed two projects for the Baidu Institute of Deep Learning.

After graduation, I joined Indeed - the world's #1 job site as a software engineer in October 2016 and transitioned to a site reliability engineer in October 2017. After spending 17 wonderful months there, I came back to China in February 2018. I first spent about half a year at Jeejio, a start-up in Beijing, leading its algorithm team. Later I joined the ByteDance AI Lab and spent another 6 months there, working on computer vision and advertising.
